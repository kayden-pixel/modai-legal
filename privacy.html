ModAI Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ModAI may collect, use, store, and protect information while providing Discord moderation, security, automation, and server-management features.

1. Information ModAI May Process

Depending on enabled features and server configuration, ModAI may process Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, message IDs, usernames, display names, command usage, moderation actions, server settings, logs, ticket/support data, reminders, automation settings, and configuration preferences.

2. Message Content

ModAI may process message content when required for enabled features such as moderation filters, anti-spam systems, command handling, ticket systems, logging, automations, or safety checks. Message content is only used to provide the bot's features and enforce configured server rules.

3. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to run commands, apply moderation actions, detect raids or spam, maintain security logs, save server settings, provide support features, improve reliability, prevent abuse, debug errors, and keep ModAI functioning correctly.

4. Data Storage

Some information may be stored so ModAI can remember server configuration, permissions, warning history, moderation logs, tickets, reminders, role settings, and other bot settings. Stored data is limited to what is needed for bot functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when required to provide ModAI's service, comply with legal obligations, investigate abuse, protect users or servers, or operate infrastructure used by the bot.

6. Data Retention

Data may be retained while the bot is active in a server or while needed for moderation, security, debugging, or support purposes. Some logs or configuration records may remain until deleted by server administrators, removed by the ModAI team, or no longer needed.

7. Data Deletion Requests

Server owners or authorized administrators may request deletion of server-related ModAI data by contacting the ModAI support team. Users may also request review or deletion of data associated with them where reasonably possible and where it does not conflict with safety, abuse-prevention, or legal requirements.

8. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children and Discord Requirements

ModAI is intended for use on Discord and follows Discord's platform requirements. Users must meet Discord's minimum age requirements to use Discord and interact with bots on Discord.

10. Third-Party Services

ModAI operates through Discord and may rely on hosting, database, logging, or infrastructure providers. Discord's own policies apply to the Discord platform and Discord account data.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when ModAI changes, when data practices change, or when platform/legal requirements change. Continued use of ModAI after updates are posted means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, support, or appeals, contact the ModAI team through the official support server: https://discord.gg/U6xr54MSR.
